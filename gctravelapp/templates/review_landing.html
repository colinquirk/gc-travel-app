To get to a review, you need the uuid. You can get this by exploring the db, e.g. with "db browser for SQLite"

When you have the uuid, go to the following url (after replacing the relevant info)

http://127.0.0.1:5000/review/[APP_TYPE]/[UUID]/[REVIEW NUMBER]

app type should be either 'basic' or 'advanced'

review number can just be '1' (this is for tracking the number of reviews on an application later)
